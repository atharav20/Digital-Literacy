Social Media Do's and Don'ts

DO's:
1) Use respectful and polite language while interacting with others
2) Keep strong passwords by using mix of charecters,numbers and special characters
3) Keep your personal information safe by not sharing it with anyone 
4) Check facts before posting anything and use verified sources
5) Try to maintain a positive and professional image online

DON'Ts:
1) Do not share sensitive personal details like passwords
2) Avoid posting content that are inappropiate and offensive
3) Do not spread false or unverified information
4) Do not use rude or offensive language
5) Do not get involved in unnecessary online arguments
